Cashew Soup

Description

Cashew Soup is a dish that I make better than most other people. The reason my Cashew Soup is better than almost everyone else's, is that I don't overcomplicate it.

Ingredients

  • 500g Vegetables
  • 1-2dl Cashews (or 1 handful)
  • 1 Herbamare Buillion

  • 1 Garlic Clove

Steps

  1. Roughly cut and clean Vegetables, Ginger and Chili. Crush and chop Garlic. Add to water with Stock and Cashews.
  2. Boil until vegetables tender, then blend it finely.
