Chilli con Carne

Description

Chilli con Carne is a dish that I make better than most other people. The reason my Chilli con Carne is better than almost everyone else's, is that I exclusively use red or orange ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cn Red Kidney Beans
  • 400-500g Beef Mince
  • 1 cn Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 cn Tomato Passata
  • 250g Carrots
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 White Onion
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Â¼ 70%+ Dark Chocolate
  • 1 Red Chili
  • 2 Beef Stock Cubes
  • Cayenne, Paprika and Cumin (ground)
  • 250ml Water
  • OPTIONAL: 25-50g finely cut tumeric slices

Steps

  1. Fry finely chopped Garlic and Onions in extra virgin olive oil. Once done, add 50/50 Chopped Tomatoes and Passata. Bring to a boil then let simmer.
  2. Sear (low-mid heat) Mince in olive oil until fat is gone, then season with Cayenne, Paprika and Cumin.
  3. Put 2 Stock Cubes, Mince, diced Carrots and Bell Peppers, and Water in the tomato sauce, bring to a boil and let simmer for 50 min.
  4. Add Kidney Beans, then simmer for another 10 min
  5. Add roughly chopped Dark Chocolate and finely cut Red Chili towards the end
